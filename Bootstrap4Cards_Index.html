Professor X

Professor Charles Francis Xavier, founder of the X-Men. Powers: world's most powerful telepath, astral projection, mind control, illusion casting, memory manipulation, psychic blasts, sensing other mutants.

Cyclops

Scott Summers. Summers is the longtime leader of the X-Men. Powers: Can project a destructive beam of ruby-colored energy force from his eyes.

Wolverine

Powers: Sharp adamantium claws, indestructible metal adamantium bonded to his skeleton, allowing enhanced physical attributes.

Beast

Dr. Henry Philip "Hank" McCoy. The scientist on the team. Powers:superhuman strength, speed, night vision, claws,stamina, agility, reflexes, and mass. Ambidextrous with his hands and feet.

Nightcrawler

Kurt Wagner. Son of Mystic and former catholic priest. Powers: Line of sight teleportation, invisibility in shadows, night vision, and wall-crawling.

Magneto

Max "Magnus" Eisenhardt. X-Men longtime enemy. Powers: Able to generate and control magnetic fields for manipulating metal, flight, force fields, electrical bolts, and electromagnetic radiation.
